Today: 18.March.2021

Recently I realised that it wasn't my fault not being able to deeply & intuitively understand the relationship between "lightness" and "brightness" of a colour... and the struggle I had understanding the whole concept was reasonably a human thing!
It was when I learnt about HCL colour space. A cylindrical transformation of cartesian coordinates of CIE Lab/Luv colour space... so brilliant! intuitive & perceptually linear...
I should admit that the initial friction made me a bit loath to experiment with colours... but now that I know what to do & how to do, I'm over the moon!
